## ü§ñ Creating Your First AI Trading Bot

### Step 1: Navigate to AI Trading
1. Click on **"AI Trading"** in the navigation menu
2. Select the **"Create Bot"** tab
3. You'll see the bot creation form

### Step 2: Configure Your Bot
1. **Bot Name**: Choose a descriptive name (e.g., "My First Bitcoin Bot")
2. **Trading Strategy**: 
   - **Trend Following**: Good for beginners, follows market trends
   - **Momentum**: More aggressive, captures price movements
   - **Mean Reversion**: Buys low, sells high based on average prices
3. **AI Model**: 
   - **DeepSeek R1**: Free, good performance
   - **Gemini 2.0 Flash**: Free, fast responses
   - **GPT-4**: Premium, most advanced (requires API key)
4. **Risk Level**:
   - **Low**: Safer trades, smaller potential gains/losses
   - **Medium**: Balanced approach (recommended)
   - **High**: More aggressive, higher potential returns and risks
5. **Max Trade Amount**: Set in AUD (start with $1,000)

### Step 3: Create and Activate
1. Click **"Create AI Trading Bot"**
2. Your bot appears in the bot list
3. Toggle the switch to **"Active"** to start trading
4. Your bot will start analyzing the market every 30 seconds

### Step 4: Monitor Performance
1. Watch your bot's real-time decisions in the **"Audit Log"** tab
2. See performance metrics update live
3. Check your portfolio balance changes

## üìö Trading Strategies Explained

### Trend Following
- **How it Works**: Identifies and follows market trends
- **Best For**: Stable markets with clear directions
- **Risk Level**: Low to Medium
- **Time Frame**: Medium to long-term trades

### Momentum Trading
- **How it Works**: Captures rapid price movements
- **Best For**: Volatile markets with strong moves
- **Risk Level**: Medium to High
- **Time Frame**: Short to medium-term trades

### Mean Reversion
- **How it Works**: Buys when price is below average, sells when above
- **Best For**: Range-bound markets
- **Risk Level**: Low to Medium
- **Time Frame**: Short to medium-term trades

### Breakout Trading
- **How it Works**: Trades when price breaks through support/resistance
- **Best For**: Markets approaching key levels
- **Risk Level**: Medium to High
- **Time Frame**: Short-term trades

## üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Bot Not Trading
**Possible Causes**:
- Bot is deactivated (check the toggle switch)
- Insufficient confidence in AI analysis
- Market conditions don't meet strategy criteria
- Maximum trade amount is too high for current balance

**Solutions**:
- Ensure bot is activated
- Lower the confidence threshold in settings
- Try a different strategy
- Reduce maximum trade amount

#### Slow Performance
**Possible Causes**:
- High server load
- Slow internet connection
- Browser cache issues

**Solutions**:
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check internet connection

#### AI Analysis Errors
**Possible Causes**:
- API rate limits reached
- Invalid market data
- AI model temporarily unavailable

**Solutions**:
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Switch to a different AI model
- Check the audit log for specific error messages

### Getting Help
1. **Check Audit Logs**: Often contains specific error information
2. **Restart Bot**: Deactivate and reactivate the bot
3. **Try Different Settings**: Adjust risk level or strategy
4. **Contact Support**: Use the help section for complex issues
